# Risk_and_Claim_Insurance_Analysis_Power-BI

## Overview
This project presents an Insurance Analytics Dashboard built using Power BI to analyze
customer demographics, policy performance, premium distribution, and claim trends.
The dashboard enables stakeholders to gain insights into insurance operations and
supports data-driven decision-making.

---

## Project Objectives
- Analyze customer distribution across policy types and demographics
- Evaluate premium and claim performance by region and city
- Identify high-risk regions using loss ratio analysis
- Understand seasonal, quarterly, and yearly claim trends
- Support strategic planning and operational optimization

---

## Dataset Information
The dataset includes insurance records with the following attributes:
- Customer demographics (Age Group, Gender)
- Policy details (Policy Type, Policy Status)
- Financial metrics (Premium Amount, Claim Amount)
- Geographic data (Region, City)
- Time dimensions (Year, Quarter, Month)

---

## Tools & Technologies
- Power BI
- Power Query (ETL and data transformation)
- DAX (calculated measures and KPIs)
- Microsoft Excel (data preprocessing)

---

## Dashboard Highlights
- Customer Analysis by policy type, gender, age group, and region
- Premium Analysis including total, average, and maximum premium
- Claims Analysis by policy type, gender, region, city, and time
- Loss Ratio comparison across regions
- Time-series analysis (yearly, quarterly, monthly trends)

---

## Key Insights & Outcomes
- Life insurance generates the highest total premium among all policy types
- Claim distribution is balanced across genders
- Certain regions exhibit higher loss ratios, indicating higher risk exposure
- Claims show seasonal patterns across months and quarters
- Middle-aged customers represent the largest share of policyholders
- Regional and city-level analysis helps identify high-claim areas
